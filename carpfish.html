How to play

  • 2048: arrow keys or swipe. Combine tiles.
  • Snake: arrow keys or swipe/touch. Avoid hitting yourself.
  • Breakout: move mouse/touch to control paddle.
  • Memory: click cards to reveal pairs.
  • Whack-a-Mole: tap moles quickly before time runs out.
  • Flappy: tap/click or Space to flap.
